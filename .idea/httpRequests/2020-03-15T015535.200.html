Introducing OpenAudience™

OpenAudience is the people-based marketing platform for the open web. The first of its kind capability to unlock walled garden advertising efficiency and ease across the entirety of the open web has been engineered around three core pillars:

Privacy

A platform built on privacy by design principles.

Knowledge

Unify data with unparalleled accuracy and precision. Unlock true knowledge about your customers or your audience.

Performance

Drive superior advertising performance and increase yield. All across the massive scale of the open web.

open web better than OpenX

With 10+ years running the industry’s largest and highest quality independent ad exchange, OpenX has unparalleled reach and scale across the open web.
